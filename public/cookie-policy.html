This Cookie Policy explains how Penned uses cookies and similar technologies when you visit our website or use our service.

1. What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work, to remember your preferences, and to provide information to site operators. We also use similar technologies such as local storage and pixels; in this policy we refer to all of them as "cookies."

2. Who we are

4. First-party and third-party cookies

Some cookies are set by Penned directly (first-party). Others may be set by service providers we use for functions such as analytics or payment processing (third-party). Those providers handle data according to their own privacy and cookie policies.

5. How long cookies last

Cookies are either "session" cookies, which are deleted when you close your browser, or "persistent" cookies, which remain on your device until they expire or you delete them. We use both, depending on the cookie's purpose.

6. Managing cookies

You can control and delete cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers let you refuse or remove cookies and notify you when one is set. If you disable strictly necessary cookies, parts of the service may not function. To learn how to manage cookies, check the help section of your browser. Where required by law, we will ask for your consent before setting non-essential cookies.
