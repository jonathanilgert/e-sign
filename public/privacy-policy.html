Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect personal information when you use Penned, an electronic document signing service operated by Earth Movers Network Inc.

1. Who we are

Penned is owned and operated by Earth Movers Network Inc. ("Penned," "we," "us," or "our"), located at 914 19th Avenue SE, Calgary, Alberta, Canada. We act as the data controller for the personal information described in this policy. For privacy questions, contact us at hello@penned.ca.

2. Information we collect

We collect only the information needed to provide a reliable, legally sound signing service.

CategoryExamplesWhy we collect it
Account informationName, email address, password (stored hashed)To create and secure your account

3. How we use information

  • To provide, maintain, and improve the Penned service.
  • To create and store the audit trail associated with each signed document.
  • To authenticate users and protect against fraud, abuse, and unauthorized access.
  • To send transactional messages (for example, signing requests, confirmations, and completed documents).
  • To respond to your questions and provide customer support.
  • To comply with legal obligations and enforce our Terms of Service.

We do not sell your personal information, and we do not use the contents of your documents for advertising.

4. Legal basis and consent

We collect, use, and disclose personal information with your consent or as otherwise permitted or required by applicable Canadian privacy law, including the Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA) and Alberta's Personal Information Protection Act (PIPA). By using Penned, you consent to the handling of your information as described here. You may withdraw consent at any time, subject to legal or contractual restrictions and reasonable notice, by contacting us — though doing so may prevent us from providing the service.

5. How we share information

We share personal information only as needed to run the service:

  • With other parties to a document. When you send a document for signature, the recipients you designate receive the document and related details.
  • With service providers. We use trusted vendors for hosting, email delivery, and payment processing. They may access information only to perform services on our behalf and are bound to protect it.
  • For legal reasons. We may disclose information where required by law, court order, or to protect the rights, safety, and property of Penned or others.
  • In a business transfer. If we are involved in a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, information may be transferred as part of that transaction, subject to this policy.

6. Data storage and security

We use administrative, technical, and physical safeguards designed to protect personal information against loss, theft, and unauthorized access, including encryption in transit and at rest. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security. Some of our service providers may store or process data outside of Canada, in which case the information may be subject to the laws of those jurisdictions.

7. Data retention

We retain personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the service. Completed documents and their audit trails may be retained for the period necessary to support their evidentiary value and to meet legal and record-keeping obligations. When information is no longer required, we securely delete or anonymize it.

8. Your rights

Subject to applicable law, you may:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Request deletion of your account and associated personal information.
  • Withdraw consent to certain uses of your information.

To exercise these rights, email hello@penned.ca. We will respond within the timeframes required by law.

9. Children's privacy

Penned is not directed to individuals under the age of majority in their province or territory, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from them. If you believe a minor has provided us information, contact us and we will delete it.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make material changes, we will update the "Last updated" date above and, where appropriate, notify you. Your continued use of Penned after changes take effect means you accept the revised policy.
